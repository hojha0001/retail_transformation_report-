ü§ñ How Autonomous Agents Work

End-to-End Agent Decision Flow (Example: Inventory Replenishment)

1 OBSERVE: Collect real-time signals (sales velocity, inventory levels, promotions, forecasts, lead times)
2 DECIDE: ML models evaluate scenarios and candidate actions (reorder, delay, reallocate, do nothing)
3 CONFIDENCE GATE: Calculate composite confidence score based on model accuracy, data freshness, signal strength, historical success
4 ACT: Execute automatically (>90% confidence), recommend (75-90%), or ask approval (<75%)
5 MEASURE: Track outcomes (stock-outs avoided, costs saved, accuracy)
6 LEARN: Update confidence weights and policy parameters offline (safe, no risky live updates)
